The service

This section will define the service the agreement covers. The factual description below is the product as designed; counsel turns it into a legal definition.

what is real today
  • Thready follows channels the user already reads, processes every post through an accountable AI pipeline, and turns threads into searchable research. Source: landing.html hero · README.md §5 rows 2/6/8
  • The web portal is the primary surface; the desktop app wraps the same UI; a terminal client follows a pattern verified in-house; mobile and alternative-OS clients are in development and are marketed exactly as that. Source: ../../design-system.md §7 · ../../library/platform-map.md · README.md §5 rows 15–19
[OPERATOR — legal counsel text required]

The contractual service definition, what is and is not included, and how in-development clients are treated (beta terms, preview terms, or exclusion).

Accounts and roles

what is real today
  • Accounts carry three-tier roles — Root, Account Admin, Standard — and administrative actions are recorded in an append-only audit log. Source: ../web/accounts-admin.html · README.md §5 row 13
  • Per-Account appearance overrides (colors, logo, slogan) sit behind a server-enforced WCAG-AA contrast gate; the Helix Development attribution footer is locked and persists under any white-label. Source: ../../theming.md §3/§10 · README.md §5 row 14
[OPERATOR — legal counsel text required]

Account-holder obligations: credential care, responsibility for activity under the account, admin-role responsibilities, and organizational-account terms.

Your content and connected sources

what is real today
  • Users connect channels themselves: Telegram sessions are designed-in; the Max adapter is in development and never claimed working. Source: ../web/settings.html, ../web/channels.html · README.md §5 row 3 — [GAP: 5.1]
  • Direct hashtags stay the user's own; anything the AI derives is always labelled "(derived)" and never passed off as the user's. Source: ../web/post-detail.html · README.md §5 row 5
  • Ingested media keeps sha256 checksums and renditions; a sensitive-lock exists in the asset browser design. Source: ../web/assets-browser.html · README.md §5 row 12
[OPERATOR — legal counsel text required]

Content ownership and license grant, the user's warranty that they may lawfully access and ingest each connected source, and the interplay with third-party platform terms (for example the terms of the messenger platforms a user connects).

AI-produced output

what is real today

  • When the full embedder is unavailable, search says so and hides relevance scores instead of inventing them. Source: ../web/search.html · README.md §5 row 9 — [GAP: 2.1]
[OPERATOR — legal counsel text required]

Accuracy disclaimers for AI-produced output, any required human-review language, and the intellectual-property status of generated documents and derived tags.

Availability and support

what is real today
  • Thready is an MVP in active design and development; no store listing is live and no installer is published today. Source: download.html honesty banner · README.md §5 row 26
  • Operations are documented: daily full backups plus hourly database incrementals, recovery-point objective about one hour, recovery-time objective about four, with a documented restore runbook. Source: ../../../deployment/index.md §1 (Q41/Q45) · README.md §5 row 24
[OPERATOR — legal counsel text required]

Whether any service level is promised or expressly not promised, support channels and hours, and maintenance-window language. The operational facts above are documentation, not a service-level commitment.
